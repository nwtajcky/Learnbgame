Addons

DECALmachine tries to work seamlessly with as many other addons as possible.

GroupPro

GroupPro makes working with huge scenes and lots of objects easy and unlocks the potential of collection instances in Blender.

Bartosz Styperek: WordPress, Twitter
Buy: Gumroad
Discuss: Blender Artitst

Batch Ops™2

Batch Ops™2 is a highly sophisticated data manager. It can outline/arrange/control all your modifiers, materials, collections (and other data) in the currently open file, scene or among the visible objects.

MOTH3R aka Ivan Santic: Web, Twitter
Buy: Gumroad, Blender Market
Discuss: Blender Artists

Hard Ops

Hard Ops is a toolkit aimed at assisting users with everything from modelling to rendering, with a focus on hard surface design.

masterXeon1001/TeamC(AR, proxe and more): Web, Twitter
Buy: Gumroad, Blender Market
Discuss: Blender Artists

MACHIN3tools

MACHIN3tools is a free, continuously evolving collection of blender tools and pie menus in a single customizable package.

Get: Gumroad, GitHub
Buy: Blender Market
Discuss: Blender Artists
